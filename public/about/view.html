About

Tessellated Security is a home security system aimed at the tinkerer, the do-it-yourself-er, and the hobbyist. By offering an ulra lowcost service, home security is attainable to anyone with wifi internet, a smartphone, and the desire to play around, a little, with a tessel.

This site serves as the point where users of the service can register an account, register their tessel device(s), and update their information.

By first registering an account here, users then can get the information necessary to set up their own tessels as security systems.

Once a tessel is set up with the system, anytime a magnetic switch opens that's connected to the tessel, the user will receive an email notifying him or her that the door has opened.

Future planned features:

  • Attaching a webcam to the tessel to pipe photos and eventually videos of intruders via email to users
  • Storing photos and videos both locally on the tessel via thumbdrive and within users' accounts on the Tessellated Security site
  • Being able to arm and disarm your tessel via number pad or switch, whichever you prefer for your security concerns
  • Setting up the tessel without using NPM
  • Integrating a payment system and subscriptions into the site
  • And much more!
